Author: Milena Liao Date: November 19, 2019  11:12:41 Category: Paper Notes

Path consistency(PC)

More constraints are assumed together!
Every (v0,vm) is SAT, all neighbourhood (vi,vi+1) are SAT.
But do not guarantee all the constraints on the path are SAT.

PC and paths of length2

Only the paths of length 2 can be explored (v0,vn-1,vn), CSP is PC iff every path of length 2 is PC.

PC and AC

PC impies AC.
Arc(i,j) is AC if Path(i,j,i) is Path.

  • AC removes incompatible values from domains.
  • PC removes pairs of values.
